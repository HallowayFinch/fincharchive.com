# Room 01

Internal name: Room 01  
Public name: The Archive  
Canonical time: 22:22

## Camera

Three-quarter overhead, approximately 25 degrees. The viewer should feel like they are standing at the desk, not looking straight down from above.

## Lighting

Warm tungsten desk lamp with cool moonlight entering from the left. No overhead light.

## Permanent environment

The desk, lamp, wall, window, corkboard, shelves, filing cabinet, chair, and stopped analog wall clock belong to the background plate.

## Interactive props

Only important archive objects should become separate layered assets: case files, field notes, recorder, photographs, keys, cassette, envelope, and future recovered objects.

## Guiding rule

Nothing exists because it looks cool. Everything exists because Finch left it there.
