FINCH ARCHIVE

Recovered notes from incomplete transmissions.

The Finch Archive catalogs incomplete transmissions, observational fragments, and recovered notes attributed to Halloway Finch. Entries are preserved as they were found: unverified, out of sequence, and occasionally in disagreement with one another.

Patterns appear, collapse, and return. Some records reference events that have not yet occurred. Readers are encouraged to note what they can, in case the record changes.
